3. Do you offer home delivery?

▼

Yes, we offer home delivery services for all our products. You can check the delivery options during checkout.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment options like UPI and wallets.

Yes, you can customize your cake by selecting your preferred flavor, design, and message during the order process.
